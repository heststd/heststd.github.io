— How it works

Three rules.
That's the game.

01

Watch the target

A target color appears at the top of the screen. That's the only color you should slice this round.

02

Slice the right color

Drag your finger across falling balls that match. Slice the wrong one, lose a heart.

03

Build the combo

Chain consecutive hits. The multiplier climbs. Your score goes vertical.
